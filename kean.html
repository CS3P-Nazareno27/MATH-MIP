Hints:


Across:
1. A mathematical statement asserting the equality of two expressions.
2. A symbol that represents a quantity that can change or vary.
3. A visual representation of data or mathematical functions, typically plotted on a coordinate plane.

Down:
1. Its graph forms a parabola, and it can have one or two real solutions.
2. The point where a line or curve intersects with one of the axes of a coordinate plane.
3. An equation whose graph forms a straight line when plotted on a coordinate plane.
