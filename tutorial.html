MathScapes Tutorial

Game Instructions

  1. Objective: Solve crossword puzzles by entering correct mathematical answers.
  2. Gameplay:
    • Choose a level from the main menu.
    • Complete each character's crossword puzzle and then proceed to the next level.
    • Each level increases in difficulty.
  3. Controls:
    • Use your keyboard to enter answers.
    • Click on level links to navigate.
  4. Hints:
    • Use your math knowledge to find solutions.
    • Each character represents a different difficulty level.
  5. Completion: Finish all levels to complete the game.
